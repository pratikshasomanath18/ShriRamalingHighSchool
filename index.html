    Academics

    Our Curriculum Overview
    01

    Marathi

    Marathi is taught in schools to help students connect with their regional language, culture, and traditions. It enhances their reading, writing, and speaking skills, improving their ability to communicate effectively in daily life. Through the study of Marathi literature and history, students also develop a deeper appreciation for moral values and cultural heritage.

    02

    English

    English is taught to help students develop strong communication skills in reading, writing, listening, and speaking. It also enhances their vocabulary and grammar, enabling them to express ideas clearly. Through literature, students gain a deeper understanding of human emotions, cultures, and values.

    03

    Kannada

    Kannada is taught to preserve and promote the rich heritage of Karnataka’s language and culture. It helps students strengthen their language skills and appreciate regional literature. The subject also instills pride in local traditions and history.

    04

    Maths

    Mathematics is taught to build logical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and numerical understanding. It forms the foundation for many day-to-day activities and future careers. Learning maths helps students develop precision, discipline, and analytical skills.

    05

    Science

    Science encourages curiosity and a spirit of exploration by helping students understand the world around them. It teaches important concepts through experiments and observations. The subject promotes critical thinking and lays the groundwork for technological and medical advancements.

    06

    Social Science

    Social Science is taught to help students understand society, history, geography, and civics. It builds awareness about the world, cultures, and governance systems. The subject also nurtures responsible citizenship and an appreciation for national unity and diversity.

    Passion for Sports and Athletics

    Our students have the freedom to explore their passion for sports, choosing activities they love.

    We encourage active participation in various competitions, from inter-school to national levels. This fosters teamwork, discipline, and excellence as they strive to reach new heights in their athletic pursuits.

    Celebrating the Arts and Creativity

    Our students are encouraged to explore their artistic talents in areas like dance, drama, and painting.

    Guided by a team of seasoned professionals, we provide opportunities for creative expression through exhibitions, concerts, and performances. These experiences empower our students to embrace their unique abilities and thrive in the world of performing arts and music.

    Embracing the World of Music

    Our students have the opportunity to explore and develop their musical talents in an inspiring environment.

    With expert guidance, we nurture their skills through lessons, performances, and concerts, enabling them to express themselves and build confidence. Our commitment to music education empowers students to discover their unique sound and thrive as young musicians.
